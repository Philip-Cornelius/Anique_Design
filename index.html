About

Everything we sell is handcrafted for all people from all walks of life.

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Anique Design was born from a desire to bring about smiles through creative designs. People should feel special and loved, and this applies not only to the recipient of our products, but also to the customer who gifts our unique products to a loved one - a feeling of excitement to enrich and satisfy.

  • Symbolism of the Butterfly

    The butterfly is a symbol of what can be achieved when we trust ourselves, and the power of transformation. Growth, development, change, and renewal are what our founder and CEO, Anita, has in sight now that her younglings have left the house to make their own way in the world. The butterfly also signifies beauty, endurance, and love, which can be found in all our designs.

  • Our Goal

    Our products will not only be aesthetically pleasing, unique, exclusive, and functional, but blessings upon the receivers.

  • Our Focus

    We focus on the personal aspects and avoid passionless mass-production, to build a community on the value of artistic inspiration merged with love.
